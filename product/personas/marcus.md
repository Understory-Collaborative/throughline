# Persona: Marcus

**Status: Draft, needs community review.** Built from a seed for First Step Out.
Validate with formerly incarcerated collaborators before treating as settled.

## Who he is

Marcus is 34, in Tarrant County, Texas. He was released from TDCJ three weeks
ago after a four-year sentence. He is sleeping on his sister's couch in
Arlington. He worked HVAC before, and he has a job lined up if he can get an
I-9 done.

## What he has

- His old paper Social Security card from years ago
- His sister's address, where he sleeps on the couch and is not on the lease

## What he does not have

- A current Texas state ID, which expired during his sentence
- A birth certificate
- Proof of residency in his own name

## What he needs from First Step Out

Help knowing what to bring to DPS. What counts, what does not, and what to do
about the residency gap. He cannot afford a wasted trip.

## The blocker

DPS requires specific documents to prove identity, citizenship, and residency.
Marcus is not sure which of the ones he has will count. The DPS website is
written at a college level, and he is not reading it fast under stress.

## The three problem layers

- **External.** The DPS office requires specific documents. He is unsure which
  of the ones he has qualify, or what to do about the gaps.
- **Internal.** He feels humiliated by needing to ask his sister for
  everything. He is anxious about wasting bus fare on a trip that ends in being
  turned away. Each office that turns him away repeats the message that he is
  not really a person yet.
- **Philosophical.** If you have done your time, the path back to ordinary life
  should not require decoding government compliance language to know what is in
  your wallet.

## Reading level

3rd to 4th grade. Marcus can read. He cannot navigate bureaucratic language
fast while under stress.

## What First Step Out gives him

A personalized, plain-language printable. Here is what to bring to DPS. Here is
what you have. Here is what you still need. Here is where to get it. The DPS
REAL ID Checklist, made human.

## Honest scope

First Step Out v1 helps Marcus walk into DPS with the right plan. It does not
handle the whole document chain. It is honest about what it cannot do. It cannot
get him his birth certificate, and it tells him plainly where to get one. It
hands off to real people where that helps.
